    Our Feature

    High-Quality Knitting & design

    Leveraging advanced knitting technology with Jacquard Multi gauge and 12GG machines. Capable of producing a wide range of sweater styles and designs to meet diverse client needs.

    Sustainable manufacturing

    ETP PLANT Capacity is 10M3 (Per hour)

    Cutting-Edge technology

    Equipped with a comprehensive range of machinery including Winding Machines, Dial Linking Machines, Jacquard Machines, Sewing Machines, and Finishing Machines for efficient and accurate production

    Ethical & Fair Trade Practices:

    Ensured workers benefit as per local regulatory laws and customers' advice. Ensured minimum wages and benefits for workers & employees. Free medical consultation given around the week by a qualified medical practitioner. Child labor strictly prohibited.

    Fast & Efficient Production:

    High monthly production capacity of 6,50,000 pieces. Streamlined operations with a skilled workforce and efficient merchandising and sampling teams. (Inferred from workforce size and team descriptions)

    Strict Quality Control

    In-house Quality Assurance Team conducts thorough inspections at various stages (ILC, IPC, MPC & FRI) to ensure garments meet desired AQL standards. Fabric/Yarn Quality Testing implemented to eliminate quality problems before manufacturing.
